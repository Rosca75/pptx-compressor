Part Format Size Usage

Embedded fonts are stored uncompressed and cannot be shrunk — only removed. Tick the fonts you do not need travelling inside the file.

Removing an embedded font means anyone who opens the deck without that font installed sees a substitute — spacing and appearance may shift. Keep brand fonts embedded; the big wins are usually large fallback faces such as Arial Unicode MS.
